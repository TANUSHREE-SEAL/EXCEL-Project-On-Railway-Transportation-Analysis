# EXCEL-Project-On-Railway-Transportation-Analysis
**Railway Transportation Analysis Dashboard 🚆**

**Project Overview**

The Railway Transportation Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze railway ticket sales, passenger transaction trends, revenue performance, travel routes, and payment behaviors.
This dashboard helps stakeholders understand operational performance, customer purchasing patterns, and the busiest railway routes through dynamic visual analytics.

**The report provides a centralized overview of:**

Total revenue generated
Total ticket transactions
Average revenue per transaction
Monthly and daily revenue trends
Most active railway routes
Ticket category performance
Payment method distribution

**Objective**

**The main objective of this dashboard is to:**
Monitor railway business performance
Identify high-performing routes
Analyze customer ticket preferences
Track revenue generation trends over time
Understand payment behaviors
Support data-driven operational decisions

**Dashboard Features**

**1. KPI Cards**

**The top section displays important business metrics:**

Total Revenue: £7,41,921
Total Transactions: 31,653
Average Revenue: £23.44

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of the railway system’s overall performance.

**2. Revenue By Month**

This bar chart visualizes monthly revenue trends.

**Insights:**

January generated the highest revenue.
February experienced a slight decline.
Revenue recovered again during March and April.
December showed minimal revenue contribution.
Purpose:

**Helps identify:**

Seasonal demand patterns
High and low revenue months
Business growth trends over time

**3. Top 5 Busiest Routes**

The pie chart highlights the railway routes with the highest passenger activity.

**Major Routes:**

Manchester Piccadilly → Liverpool Lime Street
London Euston → Birmingham New Street
London Kings Cross → York
London Paddington → Reading
London St Pancras → Birmingham New Street

**Purpose:**
Identifies heavily used travel corridors
Helps improve route planning and scheduling
Assists in resource allocation and capacity management

**4. Ticket Type Analysis**

This visual compares revenue generated by different ticket categories.

**Ticket Categories:**

Advance
Anytime
Off-Peak

**Insights:**

Off-Peak tickets generated the highest revenue.
Advance tickets also contributed significantly.
Anytime tickets produced comparatively moderate revenue.

**Purpose:**

Understand customer ticket preferences
Optimize pricing strategies
Improve promotional campaigns

**5. Revenue By Day**

The treemap displays revenue distribution across weekdays.

**Insights:**

Weekend days like Saturday and Sunday contributed strong revenue.
Thursday and Friday also showed higher performance.
Midweek days had relatively balanced revenue contributions.

**Purpose:**

Analyze daily passenger demand
Improve staffing and train scheduling
Support operational planning

**6. Payment Method Analysis**

This horizontal bar chart shows the most preferred payment methods.

**Payment Types:**

Credit Card
Contactless
Debit Card

**Insights:**

Credit Card payments dominated transactions.
Contactless payments were the second most preferred.
Debit Card usage was comparatively lower.

**Purpose:**

Understand customer payment behavior
Enhance payment infrastructure
Improve customer convenience

**7. Revenue By Ticket Type**

The donut chart represents the percentage contribution of each ticket category toward total revenue.

**Key Observation:**

Advance tickets hold the largest revenue share.
Anytime and Off-Peak contribute significantly as well.

**Purpose:**

Provides a quick visual comparison of ticket revenue distribution.
